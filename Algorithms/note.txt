### Bitwise Operator

a: 00111100 (60)
b: 00001101 (13)

left shift 2 digits: 60 -> 240 => 60 * 2 ^2
Right shift 2 digits: 60 -> 15 => 60 / (2 ^ 2)

^ bit wise XOR: (if one is 0 the other is 1, return 1; else return 0)
a ^ b = 00110001
